Mornings can be a stressful time. Getting ready for work. Getting your kids ready for school. So what if – at least when it came to making sure your kids didn’t miss the bus – you could remove any added stress? No more worrying at the office if they’re standing in the dark, or cold, for an untold amount of time. No more worry if they’ve made it to school safely. And no more worry in the afternoons, wondering if they’ve made it home safely.

 

Features for Parents

Bus Arrival Notifications:

Parents will recive advance intimations on their app to be ready for their child pick up, ensuring hassle free coordination. No need for the buss to wait

Easy For Day Marking

Parents can simply click to mark students' day off to avoid unecessary reminder calls on off day, also streamlined trip planning by bus driver.

Child Pick Up From School:

-Now parents are able to see if thier child is has been seated in the bus right away

Real Time Tracking

Buss will be real time tracked via bus driver's app and they will not be allowed t turn off their location tracking. Upon turning of location, an intimation will be sent.

Exceptional Notifications

All sort of incedents which cause any delay in transport of the child to and from schools such as traffic delays and buss going off-route will be sent to the parent as well.

Features for School

Bincee Portal

Profiles of drivers and students which helps school transport managers to communicate better.
Further, it provides school with import Features which makes the data entry prcess smooth and simple.

  

Fleet Tracking

Real time tracking of busses and status updates of student, alerts on traffic jams and emergency situations. Through fleet tracking system schools will not be needing any traditional hardware such as GPS tracking.

Features of Drivers

Bincee is a solution that work as a blessing for Drivers as Parents will be well informed about the bus delay through real time alerts and can adjust their routies accordingly

Bincee helps drives to:
  • Optimize routes automatically through Smart Routing on daily traffic controls.
  • Route sugestion in case of unusual traffic sittuations.
  • know each stoppages with the list of students at each stop.
  • Notify parents and students in advance of bus arival, so no useless delays and wait for bus drivers as well.
  • Notify parents and schools in case of Emergency alerts.
